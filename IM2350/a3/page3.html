NEON LUCY




Infared hieroglyphics,

neon shaped suspended

The glowing silhouette visits,

a ghoul dancing in the eye.

Ephemeral light caresses,

can't judge the distance,

eons away while in the mind.



Her glow is effervescent,

electric ghost my witness.

Hazy phantom of dreams,

a sheen to feel clean.



Wishing to visit,

the eternal space she occupies,

redefining concepts of divine.

A temptress in lambent lights,

her seductive whispers,

heard through gaps of time.



Floating shapes in the matrix,

sacred geometry aligns.

Particles of dim existence,

burned into synapses,

serotonin depleted in time.



Cherising the shape of her,

the only light that shines through

Then watch it fade to black,

glowing beacon of a past.



Nebular metamorphosis,

star death eclipsing the third-eye.

Primordial ooze of melted dreams,

coursing through veins, fractals shaping an infinite maze.



Cerebral vacany,

dark matter all that's left.

Soul filled to the brim,

overflowing with grim.



Teleported and contorted,

by Lucy etched in gray matter.

warmping all sinews,

pastel scenic views,

emitted behind watercolour eyes.



Cortex wiped to default,

erased by space and time.


Offering the last fragment,


of peace of mind.
